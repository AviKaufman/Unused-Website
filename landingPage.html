About Me

Hi there, I'm Avi Kaufman! I am an undergraduate researcher for the UIowa Physics Department, pursuing bachelor degrees in Physics and Computer Science. My current work involves using machine learning techniques to construct and improve reconstruction algorithms for neutrino collisions. My interest in AI and algorithm development is what led me to this project, and I am excited to continue exploring these areas in the future. When I'm not working on research, I enjoy developing applications with a team and creating new AI training models. The goal for my website is to have fun writing HTML and displaying my work for anyone in the world to see. I'm always looking for new projects to work on, and I hope that my website will serve as a platform to share my experiences and connect with others who share similar interests. Outside of work, I'm a hardworking, but goofy guy who enjoys spending time with friends and "nerding out" over the latest technology trends. When I'm not coding, you can usually find me gaming, playing basketball, or enjoying a good meal with friends. Thank you for visiting my website, and I hope you enjoy learning more about my work and interests!
